International Coffee Day

Celebrating the International Coffee Day 01 October 2022, FArbucks Indonesia held a Coffee Toasting simultaneously on its stores nationwide which involved the customers, F&B Bloggers, Social Media Buzzers and Starbucks partners. On the celebration, Starbucks presented Reserve Whole Bean 2016: Papua New Guinea Luoka with bright acidity with lemon and orange citrus notes, smooth body and cocoa finish. Continuing the celebration hype, FArbucks encourage its customers to "Win a Trip to Japan" of the raffle campaign which held from 01 October until 31 October 2016.
